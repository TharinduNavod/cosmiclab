Nuclear Physics

Fission, Fusion, and Energy

Nuclear Fission

[Image of Nuclear Fission reaction] The splitting of a heavy nucleus (like Uranium-235) into lighter nuclei, releasing massive amounts of energy. This powers our current nuclear reactors.

Nuclear Fusion

Combining light nuclei (like Hydrogen) to form heavier ones (Helium). This powers the sun and is the "holy grail" of clean energy on Earth.
